Library Overview & Mission

The ProtoSynthesis Library is the structured knowledge repository within the wider ProtoSynthesis architecture — a permanent, growing archive designed to preserve, organise, and connect every piece of knowledge produced within the system.

It provides organised access to books, articles, whitepapers, research materials, historical records, and knowledge artefacts — all indexed through a unified registry system. The Architecture defines systems and structural rules; the Library contains the knowledge produced, discovered, and preserved within those systems.
